Fiction - Short Stories

For Love and Kisses

By Andrea Pasion-Flores

PHP 300.00

Andrea Pasion’s For Love and Kisses is a collection of seven stories: “For Love and Kisses,” “Vanessa Calling,” “Buttercups,” “Skin Art,” “Love in Mini Stops,” “The Hungry Ghost,” and “How to Drink Whisky, If You’re a Girl,” all of which are on Filipinas of different ages and walks of life. The stories highlight friendships, marriage and love affairs, and betrayal and loss, drawing the reader into the world of women, until it becomes the reader’s own.

Andrea Pasion-Flores was born in Manila. She is a graduate of the University of the Philippines where she received her degrees in Journalism, Law, and her MA in Creative Writing. Her fiction has appeared in the Philippines Graphic, Philippines Free Press, the UP Institute of Creative Writing’s Likhaan, and Silliman University’s Sands and Coral journals.
