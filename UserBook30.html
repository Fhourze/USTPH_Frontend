Isinalarawang Introduksiyon sa Arkitekturang Filipino

Rino D.A. Fernandez, Kristina M.A. Banzon

PHP 100.00

A sequel to the bestselling Diksiyonaryong Biswal ng Arkitekturang Filipino, Isinalarawang Introduksiyong sa Arkitekturang Filipino walks us through the different architectural styles from different time periods and their cultural and historical origins. Accompanied by illustrations, the book introduces its readers, especially aspiring architects, to the vast nature of the architecture of our land.

Rino D.A. Fernandez is a faculty member of the College of Architecture at the University of Santo Tomas, Manila; former Dean of the College of Architecture and Fine Arts of Aquinas University of Legazpi (now UST-Legazpi), Albay and former Academic Coordinator of Escuela Taller - Intramuros, a school dedicated to conservation and restoration funded by Agencia Española Cooperacion Internacional para el Desarollo (AECID). He is a fellow of the United Architects of the Philippines (UAP), and Vice Head of the International Network on Traditional Building, Architecture and Urbanism (INTBAU-Philippines), and a member of a scientific committee for International Research - the Journal on Traditional Building, Architecture and Urbanism (JTBAU). Architect Fernandez hails from Legazpi City, Albay. Kristina M.A. Banzon is an architect in Sweden and the Philippines. She is a contributor to the book Diskyunaryong Biswal: Visual Dictionary on Filipino Architecture. She has a Bachelor of Science degree in Architecture from the University of Santo Tomas. She holds a Master’s Degree in Innovation - Business, Engineering, and Design from Linnaeus University Sweden. She is a Swedish Institute alumna, scholar, and local board member. She is also an alumna of the Engelsberg Summer School in Classical Architecture in Sweden. She was a representative of the International Network on Traditional Building, Architecture and Urbanism (INTBAU-Philippines) to Sweden as an Ax:son Johnson scholar.
