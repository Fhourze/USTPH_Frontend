Mariposa Gang and Other Stories

Katherine Torres

PHP 280.00

Deceptive and devastating, Catherine Torres’ Mariposa Gang and Other Stories tells the story of Filipinos whose dreams are crushed by unfortunate social reality and are forced to leave their homeland to search for a better life on foreign shores. Repleting with themes of loneliness, feelings of alienation, and rootlessness, her stories paint the authenticity of human life and its fleeting nature in the most familiar and nostalgic sense.

Catherine Torres, writer and diplomat, was born in Manila in 1980. Her stories have won Palanca Awards and appeared in journals and anthologies in the Philippines, Singapore, and the United States. She studied Psychology at the University of the Philippines and worked briefly in the corporate world and in media. In 2004, she joined the Foreign Service, which has taken her to postings in New Delhi, Singapore, and now, Berlin. She has helped her husband, a Korean scholar, translate Korean literary works into English, but considers their son, Samuel, their best project yet. When world affairs and her writing schedule permit, Cathy enjoys watching the waves and playing the ukulele. Her YA novel Sula’s Voyage was released by Scholastic Asia in 2016.
