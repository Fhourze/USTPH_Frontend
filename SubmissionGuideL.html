SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

Writers interested in publishing their works with the UST Publishing House may use this form to submit their manuscript (MS).

To complete this submission, please prepare the following:

  • The complete MS in Microsoft Word format, with table of contents, bionote/s of the author/s, editor/s, and contributors.
  • A second version of the MS without the author’s name/s, without the bionote/s, and without the acknowledgements (for the double-blind peer review).
  • A short description (100-300 words) of your MS.
  • Author/s’ or editor/s’ curriculum vitae.
  • Author/s’ or editor/s’ photo/s.
  • A cover letter addressed to Asst. Prof. Benedict B. Parfan, Director, UST Publishing House, stating your intention to submit the manuscript, and granting us permission to send the MS to reviewers.
  • For TRANSLATIONS, a certification that the original copyright holder has granted you the license to translate the work.
  • For ANTHOLOGIES, a documentary proof or individually signed certifications by your contributors permitting you to include their works in the anthology.

Please note:

  • We only accept works written in Filipino and/or English, or works with Filipino/English translations.
  • Submissions with photographs or artworks used without credits, captions, and permissions will be automatically rejected.

Privacy Notice on the personal data we collect through this form:

In accordance with data privacy laws, you are hereby informed that only the UST Publishing House administrators, staff, editorial board, and peer reviewers (for manuscripts) will have access to the information submitted, and it will only be used for official purposes such as, but not limited to, manuscript evaluation, (and if the MS is accepted for publication), filling out award nomination forms, marketing, deliveries, and other related functions.
