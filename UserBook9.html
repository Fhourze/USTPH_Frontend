Fiction - Novel

Blue Angel, White Shadow: A Novel

By Charlson Ong

PHP 400.00

Twenty-five-year-old lounge singer Laurice Saldiaga is found dead in her room at the Blue Angel Café and Bar in Binondo, Manila’s Chinatown. Inspector Cyrus Ledesma, a Chinese mestizo cop with a dark past is assigned to investigate the matter. The bar owner, Antonio Cobianco, is a known associate of Mayor Lagdameo Go-Lopez. Ledesma walks into a cauldron of intrigues, labyrinths, crumbling edifices, dogfights and suspects that include a jaded, whiskey-drinking female bar manager, a psychic journalist, a dyslexic piano player, a quick-trigger pit bull owner, an aging saxophone player, and Cyrus’ own uncle, Police Chief Ruben Jacinto.

Charlson Ong has published several collections of short fiction including Men of the East and Other Stories, Woman of AmKaw and Other Stories, Conversion and Other Fictions, A Tropical Winter’s Tale and Other Stories. He has also written novels such as Embarrassment of Riches (Philippine Centennial Literary Prize), Banyaga, A Song of War (National Book Award) and Of That Other Country We Now Speak and Other Stories. He was born, raised, and continues to live in Manila.
